They are perfect for you if...

You want to make the most of every minute of the trip to fully experience the local tradition and culture: early wake-ups, long transfers, frequent changes of accommodation don’t scare you... on the contrary, they excite you! You want that adrenaline that only an adventure trip around the world can give you! But that’s not all: you like authentic experiences because an adventure is only true if it allows you to discover new sides of the world and of yourself and to meet new people, from your travel companions to the locals. The hard part is, of course, choosing the destination – but we’re here for that, aren’t we?
