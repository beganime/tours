The Newest Continent that is not a continent: in Oceania, the main character is the ocean. The land, except for Australia, consists of tiny islands where you can breathe a sense of peace and stillness that is so far away from the hectic chaos of the rest of the world. For centuries, people born here have lived as one with the ocean, respecting the rhythms and the laws of the sea, and will continue to do so, in order to keep the natural beauty of these places pure and untouched. We should quietly arrive, and quietly we should leave - coming back home with us, the feeling of the vastness of the Australian outback, and the great adventures you can have in New Zealand.
