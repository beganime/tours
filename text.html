"Oh old continent! Burdened with years,
rough, holy, sublime mistress of the
spirit, who filters perfumes and senses!"

Dezső Kosztolányi

It is the cradle of the great ancient civilisations, of empires that sailed in gold and fell into the ashes; homeland of writers, poets and philosophers who laid the foundations of man's thought, of artists who influenced the world with their art. Center of the Earth for centuries, the great navigators set off from here towards the unknown – while now it is a popular destination for travellers from all over the globe. There are endless stories to listen to and countless hidden views to admire: every road is an adventure, every corner a discovery.
